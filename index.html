Diet Chart

Breakfast

  • Oatmeal with berries and a scoop of protein powder
  • Greek yogurt with honey, nuts, and a banana
  • Whole-grain toast with avocado and a poached egg

Mid-Morning Snack

  • A handful of almonds
  • An apple with peanut butter
  • A protein shake

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and steamed broccoli
  • Tuna salad with mixed greens and a whole-grain roll
  • Lentil soup with a side of mixed vegetables

Afternoon Snack

  • Carrot sticks with hummus
  • Cottage cheese with pineapple
  • A handful of mixed nuts

Dinner

  • Baked salmon with sweet potato and asparagus
  • Lean beef stir-fry with bell peppers and brown rice
  • Chickpea and vegetable curry with quinoa

Evening Snack (if needed)

  • A glass of warm milk with a dash of cinnamon
  • A small bowl of mixed berries
  • A small portion of dark chocolate (70% cacao or higher)

Gym Exercise Plan

Monday: Chest and Triceps

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Chest Flyes: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Cardio: 20 minutes of running or cycling

Tuesday: Back and Biceps

  • Deadlift: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Cardio: 20 minutes of running or cycling

Wednesday: Rest or Light Cardio

  • Light jog, cycling, or brisk walking for 30-45 minutes

Thursday: Legs

  • Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per leg
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Cardio: 20 minutes of running or cycling

Friday: Shoulders and Abs

  • Shoulder Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Front Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 60 seconds
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 20 reps per side
  • Cardio: 20 minutes of running or cycling

Saturday: Full Body or Active Recovery

  • Full Body Circuit (3 rounds):
    • Push-Ups: 15 reps
    • Bodyweight Squats: 20 reps
    • Dumbbell Rows: 15 reps per arm
    • Sit-Ups: 20 reps
    • Jumping Jacks: 30 seconds
  • Alternatively, engage in an active recovery activity like yoga or swimming
  • Cardio: 20 minutes of running or cycling

Sunday: Rest

  • Optional light cardio: brisk walking or gentle cycling for 30 minutes
